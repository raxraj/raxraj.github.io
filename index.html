About us

Spark Talks was founded in 2017 with a team of passionate people who wish to add value to the lives of others. We started out as a Social Media Platform to provide motivation and inspiration through success stories, quotes, poetry, articles and videos, and thereafter organising seminars and pep talks providing a platform to soak-in wisdom and inspiration from delegated speakers. What started as a simple conference back then is now a robust media platform that covers a wide array of subjects with speakers from every conceivable background, including entrepreneurship, public policy, sports, entertainment and social initiatives. Spark Talks passionately believes that a well-told story has the power to reshape attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world.
